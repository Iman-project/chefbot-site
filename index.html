CHEFBOT AI

CHEFBOT is an AI-powered restaurant assistant that works on WhatsApp.

What it does

  • Automatically responds to customers
  • Sends menus by day of the week
  • Accepts orders
  • Tracks order status
  • Handles complaints
  • Works for multiple restaurants

Problem Solved

Small restaurants struggle to manage customer orders and replies. CHEFBOT automates this.
